How to Use UniDownlaod

1

Copy Video URL

Go to YouTube and copy the URL of the video you want to download.

Example: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=...
2

Paste URL & Fetch

Paste the URL in the input field and click the arrow button to fetch video information.

Tip: Wait for the video thumbnail and qualities to load
3

Select Quality

Choose your preferred video quality from the dropdown menu.

Higher quality means larger file size
4

Download

Click the download button and wait for your video to finish downloading.

Files are saved in the downloads folder

Additional Information

  • Supports various YouTube video formats
  • Downloads highest quality audio automatically
  • No registration required
  • Free to use
